## Snapshot restore

Akash provide daily snapshots of the GravityBridge blockchain taken at midnight UTC.

|Variable|Value|
|---|---|
|`SNAPSHOT_JSON`|`https://cosmos-snapshots.s3.filebase.com/gravitybridge/snapshot.json`|
